Become a Volunteer

We’re glad you want to work with the Tacy Foundation. Becoming a volunteer is a simple process, and adult volunteers also play an important role in supporting programs and events.

  1. Becoming a Tacy Volunteer

    • Fill out the online consent forms.
    • You’ll receive an email after completing the form with a link to complete your orientation. It is all online.
    • After completing orientation, you’ll receive another email with current programs and instructions on how to sign up.
    • Some events may require an audition.
    • You can also view current programs on the Tacy Foundation website.

  2. For Parents, Relatives, and Other Awesome Adults

    • Site supervisors supervise a regular Tacy Foundation event at a hospital, senior center, or school. This is an on-site role with an average time commitment of 4 hours a week. Training is provided.
    • Program coordinators work with site supervisors to support and ensure a high-quality, consistent, and joyful program. This is a remote role with a flexible schedule and an average time commitment of 4 to 8 hours per month. Training is provided.
