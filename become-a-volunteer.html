Become a Volunteer

We’re glad you are interested in volunteering with the Tacy Foundation. Volunteers help bring music, reading, and community support to people in need, while also building valuable skills and experiences.

  1. How to Get Started

    • Complete the online registration and consent forms.
    • Attend an online orientation session after registration.
    • Review available programs and select activities that match your interests.
    • Sign up for events and follow the provided instructions.
    • Some programs may require a short audition or skill review.

  2. Volunteer Opportunities

    • Music and reading programs at hospitals, schools, and senior centers.
    • Community outreach events and performances.
    • Educational support programs for students.
    • Flexible participation based on your availability.

  3. Adult Support Roles

    • Site supervisors assist with organizing and managing on-site programs, typically about 4 hours per week.
    • Program coordinators support planning and communication, usually 4 to 8 hours per month with flexible scheduling.
    • All roles include training and ongoing support.
