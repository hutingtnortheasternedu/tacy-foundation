Educational Services

The Tacy Foundation provides educational services that support music learning, reading outreach, and community engagement through youth-centered activities.

  1. Training and Music Support

    • Guitar Pals® Training on Wednesdays, ongoing, from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.
    • Learn a song to play for seniors upon your return in Spring.
    • Make a video of your performance for the YouTube Playlist using songs in the public domain and marked “Unlisted.”

  2. Reading and Educational Outreach

    • Make a video of your reading of a children’s book in the public domain for Reading Express®.
    • Bring magazines for seniors to help them stay connected to the outside world and encourage conversation.
