Educational Services

The Tacy Foundation offers educational services that support music learning, reading outreach, and community engagement through student-centered activities.

  1. Music Training and Practice

    • Weekly music training sessions held on Wednesdays from 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m.
    • Students prepare songs to perform for seniors and community members.
    • Participants may record and share performances through approved online platforms.

  2. Reading and Outreach Programs

    • Students record readings of children’s books to support literacy programs.
    • Volunteers provide reading materials for seniors to encourage engagement and conversation.
    • Programs are designed to support both learning and community connection.
