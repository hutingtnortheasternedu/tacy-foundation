About Us

The Tacy Foundation is a nonprofit organization that uses music to support learning, connection, and community service.

The foundation encourages young musicians to share their talents through volunteer activities that bring comfort and joy to others. Through music, students can build confidence, creativity, and leadership skills while making a positive difference in the community.

The organization also helps families and communities by promoting music education and meaningful service opportunities. Its goal is to show how music can inspire hope, kindness, and personal growth.

At the Tacy Foundation, music is more than performance. It is a way to connect people, support others, and create a lasting impact.
