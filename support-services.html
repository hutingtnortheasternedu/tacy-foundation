Support Services

The Tacy Foundation offers volunteer support services that connect music, reading, and community care. The categories below show available opportunities and their schedules or availability.

  1. Creative Projects from Home

    • Make thank you cards for teachers at any time.
    • Create get well cards for hospital patients at any time.
    • Write messages of encouragement to seniors and healthcare staff at any time.

  2. Newsletter and Community Stories

    • Write about your volunteer experience for newsletters or community updates.
    • Interview volunteers or seniors to share their stories.
    • Create articles based on your experiences to support community awareness.

  3. Performance and Outreach Activities

    • Participate in music performances at hospitals, schools, or senior centers based on scheduled events.
    • Join group performances during weekends or special events.
    • Assist with organizing outreach programs and community events.
