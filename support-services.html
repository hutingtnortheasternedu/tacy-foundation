Support Services

The Tacy Foundation offers volunteer support services that connect music, reading, and community care. The categories below show available opportunities and their hours or availability.

  1. Creative Projects from Home

    • Make thank you cards for teachers as an at-home project available at any time.
    • Make get well cards for hospital patients as an at-home project available at any time.
    • Write emails of encouragement to seniors and hospitals at any time. Jokes are welcome if they are in good taste.

  2. Newsletter and Community Stories

    • Write about your Tacy Foundation experience for Facebook and the newsletter.
    • Interview volunteer directors or seniors to learn how youth, music, and service have impacted them.
    • Write an article or story for the newsletter based on your discoveries. Each story earns 1.5 hours of SSL credit.
